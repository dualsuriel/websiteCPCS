Undergraduate Research Opportunities

We offer positions in our lab for undergraduate students to gain valuable research experience. Students must be responsible, dedicated, and enthusiastic. The ability to comfortably interact with children and parents is key. Students work with children and parents, help recruit and schedule families, organize data collection, code video recordings, and more. Research assistantships for course credit are offered through the Psychology Department’s 290 program.
If you are interested in a position in our lab, please complete the form below and email it back to the Lab Coordinator at psych-cpcs@illinois.edu.

Prospective Graduate Students
